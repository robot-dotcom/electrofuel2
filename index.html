Our Story

ElectroFuel 2

Founded by four UiTM Sarawak students, ElectroFuel 2 brings innovative street food concepts to campus life. Our mission is to serve affordable, Instagram-worthy food and drinks that satisfy student cravings while providing a fast, stylish, and memorable café experience.

"To become a leading youth-focused food and beverage brand in Malaysia, known for unique product offerings and strong campus presence."
